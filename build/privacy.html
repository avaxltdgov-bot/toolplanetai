🛡️ At ToolPlanetAI, your privacy is our priority. We believe in complete transparency about how we handle your data. This policy explains what we collect, why we collect it, and how we protect it.

📋

1. Information We Collect

We collect only the minimum information necessary to provide our AI tools:

  • Text Input: Text you submit to our AI tools for processing

  • Technical Data: Browser type, device type, IP address (anonymized)
  • Cookies: Small files to improve your browsing experience

We do NOT collect: your name, email, phone number, or any personal identification information unless you voluntarily provide it.

🎯

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used exclusively for:

  • Processing your AI tool requests and returning results
  • Improving the performance and features of our tools
  • Understanding how visitors use our site to make it better
  • Ensuring the security and stability of our services

We never sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing or advertising purposes.

🤖

3. AI Processing & Third Party Services

  • GitHub Pages — Hosts our frontend website

4. Cookies Policy

We use cookies to enhance your experience on ToolPlanetAI:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for the site to function properly
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how visitors use our site
  • Preference Cookies: Remember your settings and preferences

You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time. Note that disabling cookies may affect some functionality of our site.

5. Data Security

We take data security seriously and implement the following measures:

  • HTTPS encryption for all data transmission

While we strive to protect your data, no internet transmission is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of information transmitted to our site.

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Right to Access: Request information about data we hold about you

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the details below.
